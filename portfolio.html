Ruby: Sudoku Master

This project was completed in three days. Sudoku master was written to solve Sudoku puzzles, as the name suggested. At this stage, it is capable of solving easy Sudoku problems. It doesn't make use of any AI, but a simple algorithm involves checking the validity of each value in a cell and narrowing it down to one single value. The purpose of the project is to illustrate my ability to problem-solve and how well do I know how to use Ruby language and its control structures.

Qonnect: Ruby on Rails

Qonnect is a two way marketplace, connecting interpreters and clients, to solve the problem that interpreters are underpaid and to increase a pool of clients for the interpreters, by making the service accessible to a wider community. This project is to illustrate my skills in database and Ruby on Rails. It was developed in less than two weeks.
